About


Joe is programmer, consultant, and author. He's been working in the software industry as a programmer, architect and freelancer for over 10 years. His expertise ranges from Ruby to Java to iOS. Joe has worked for companies of all sizes, including IBM with 300,000 employees and small startups with just a few people. As a result he understands the varying needs of each organization. Joe is located in Rocket City, USA.
